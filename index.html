Harry Stales

Harry Styles is a British singer-songwriter and actor. Before making his solo music, he was in the British-Irish band One Direction. In recent years he has released 3 albums: Harry Styles, Fine Line and Harry’s House. He is currently performing all over the world.

I’ve been a Harry Styles fan since 2011, I’ve followed him since his years in One Direction. Since the start of his solo career, I listen to his music every day. Through Twitter I’m in contact with other “Harries” (the name of his fans). These fans are from all over the world. Harry’s music makes me happy. Next year I will see him 3 times in the Johan Cruijff ArenA. I’m really excited to see him again, I saw Harry live in New York this year, such a unique experience that I will never forget.
